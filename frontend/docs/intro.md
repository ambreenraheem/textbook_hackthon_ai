# Welcome to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to this interactive textbook on **Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics**! This comprehensive resource is designed to take you from fundamental concepts to advanced techniques in building intelligent robotic systems.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook covers:

- **Robot Kinematics**: Understanding robot motion and positioning
- **Dynamics & Control**: Modeling forces and designing controllers
- **Perception Systems**: Computer vision and sensor fusion
- **Motion Planning**: Path planning and trajectory optimization
- **Manipulation**: Grasping and object handling
- **Locomotion**: Walking, running, and balancing
- **Human-Robot Interaction**: Safe and natural collaboration
- **AI Integration**: Machine learning for robotics
- **System Design**: Building complete robotic systems
- **Ethics & Safety**: Responsible robotics development
